Police Fatalities in United States

Introduction

Police Fatalities, a.k.a deaths caused by polices. Over the years, police brutality has always been a very big issue and this was one of the reasons why the movement Big Lives Matter was formed in 2013(Statista Research Department, 2021). One of the most controversial police fatalities incident was when an African-American man, George Floyd, was murdered by a police as the police knelt on Floyd's neck for over nine minutes while he was handcuffed(Wikipedia, n.d.).

In this visualization, we will be looking at the data of police fatalities throughout the years.

Number of police fatalities in the United States

In this combo map, we are able to visualize the number of police fatalities in the states of US per 1 million population along with the population of each state. The state that has the darkest colour, which is Wyoming, has the highest number of police fatalities per 1 million population of 795. Some states have really small circles plotted in it despite the state being very huge, Alaska is one of the best examples. Despite California being the state with the largest population, it does not have the highest number of police fatalities per 1 million population. This shows that large population doesn't necessarily mean high police fatality rate.



Police Fatalities in each day throughout each month (2000-2016)

From January to August, the overall colour of the grids are darker, which means number of police fatalities are higher from January to August. However starting from September to December, the overall color of the grid boxes are becoming lighter and the total number of police fatalities is declining starting from September. The reason for this phenomenon may be due to seasonal pattern during that time, which is Autumn.

Police Fatalities of people in different stages of life (2000-2016)

From 2000 to 2016, there is an uptrend of police fatalities regardless of which stage of life. Majority of the victims killed by police are adults, which is then followed by middle-aged adults, senior adults, teen. Despite having the movement Black Lives Matters formed in 2013, there is no obvious or significant improvement in the police fatalities. Number of police fatalities have also spiked in 2015, it is also reported that many of the victims are black men.

What weapons are the victims armed with?

Ignoring the unknowns, we can see that majority of the victims either have a knife or a gun. This shows that the victims are armed and this proves that the murdered are indeed very dangerous and does not show signs of innocence. These victims may in the end be murdered by the police due to showing signs of harming others.
